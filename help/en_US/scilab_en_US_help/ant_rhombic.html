ant_rhombic

Design of rhombic antenna

Syntax

[H,phi,l]=ant_rhombic(f,Delta,c)

Parameters

f :

frequency (in MHz)

Delta :

Angle of elevation (in degrees)

c :

Speed of light (in m/s)

H :

Rhombic height (in m)

phi :

Tilt angle (in degrees)

l :

Wire length (in m)

Description

Design of rhombic antenna

Examples

f=20;
Delta=10;
c=3*10^8;
[H,phi,l]=ant_rhombic(f,Delta,c)
